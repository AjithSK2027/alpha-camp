How to choose

The right stay for the right moment.

Camp Alpha is flexible enough for quiet weekends and active group itineraries. These quick notes help you choose faster.

Best for couples

Choose a couple room if your priority is privacy, slower mornings and a romantic estate stay.

  • Anniversary stays
  • Birthday weekends
  • Nature photography
  • Nearby scenic drives

Best for groups

Choose dormitory accommodation if the trip is about shared time, games and easy group planning.

  • Friends groups
  • Corporate outings
  • Student travel
  • Budget-conscious teams

Best for adventure

Choose camping when bonfire, open air, estate walks and the night sky matter most.

  • Camping with bonfire
  • Coffee estate camping
  • Weekend escapes
  • Outdoor games
