The estate mood

A boutique nature stay with the ease of home.

Set within the green calm of Aldur, Camp Alpha brings together the intimacy of a homestay, the playfulness of camping and the practical comfort of budget luxury.

Days begin with coffee-scented air and estate trails. Evenings soften into conversations around the bonfire, the sound of the stream and unhurried meals after a day of exploring Mullayanagiri, Baba Budangiri or hidden corners of the plantation.

  • Private coffee estate with a natural stream and group-friendly open spaces.
  • Flexible rooms, cottage, dormitories and furnished tents for mixed travel groups.
  • Hosts, better food, Wi-Fi, parking, power backup, games, music, projector and campfire.
