About Camp Alpha

A coffee estate retreat built for slower, richer weekends.

Camp Alpha brings boutique hospitality into the green quiet of Lourdes Estate, Kuduvalli, Aldur - a place where comfort, nature and direct hosting meet.

Our story

Not a resort routine. A hosted estate experience.

The best Chikmagalur stays are not rushed. They have the smell of coffee leaves after rain, the sound of water moving over stone, warm food after a long drive and enough space for families, couples and teams to feel at ease.

Camp Alpha was shaped for exactly that rhythm. Guests arrive for a luxury homestay, a camping night, a corporate outing or a family retreat, but they remember the smaller things: a quiet chair near the stream, laughter over indoor games, the first flame of the bonfire and the feeling of waking up inside a living coffee estate.

Design values

Luxury that feels natural.

Camp Alpha is positioned for guests searching for a hidden gem Chikmagalur stay, but the experience is intentionally grounded: warm, clean, scenic and personal.

Warm hospitality

Direct conversations, practical planning and a stay experience shaped around the actual guests arriving.

Nature first

Forest green views, coffee plantation texture and a riverside setting give the property its calm.

Premium simplicity

Minimal design, large photography, clean spaces and comfort without unnecessary noise.

Lourdes Estate

Aldur gives you the Chikmagalur you came for.

Camp Alpha sits at Lourdes Estate, Kuduvalli, Aldur, Chikkamagaluru, Karnataka 577133. It keeps guests close to coffee country while staying away from the crowded edges of town.

That makes it a strong choice for weekend getaway Karnataka searches, stay near Aldur queries, nature stay Chikmagalur trips and guests planning day drives to Mullayanagiri, Baba Budangiri, Hirekolale Lake and the Coffee Museum.
